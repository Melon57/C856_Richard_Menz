Entertainment and Activities

Eating

Over 100 places to eat in Taniti, including over 60 which are bars. No matter what you like Taniti has a place for you. Enjoy chinese, mexican, italian, japanese, greek, french, thai, indian, mediterranean, or local cuisines. Taniti also has many local breweries, so don't miss out on the worlds finest micro brewerie beers.

Tahini has an outdoor acitivity for anyone. Tahini houses 10 of the 13 world climate zones. All the outdoor activies such as bike, canoeing, camping, diving, fishing, hiking, horseback riding, sailing, skiing, and many more it is all here. Don't forget to check out Beach Tahiti, Mount Tahiti, or Waterfall Tahiti some of Tahiti many wonders.

About the island

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000.
